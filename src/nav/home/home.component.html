About Calintaan

Calintaan is a municipality in the province of Occidental Mindoro, Philippines. It is located on the western side of Mindoro Island, facing the South China Sea. The municipality is known for its coastal areas, agriculture, and fishing industries. Like many municipalities in the Philippines, Calintaan has its own local government unit responsible for providing basic services and governance to its residents.
