Historical Background

Calintaan is a municipality in the province of Occidental Mindoro, Philippines. It is known for its rich cultural heritage and beautiful tourist spots.

The town has a vibrant history that dates back to the early 1900s. It has grown significantly over the years, becoming a hub for local tourism and culture.

About Us

We are dedicated to promoting the beauty and culture of Calintaan. Our mission is to provide visitors with an unforgettable experience through our rich history, vibrant culture, and stunning tourist spots.
